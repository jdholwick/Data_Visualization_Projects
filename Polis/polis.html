Greek Polis Data

Hypothesis: The more Greek (or Hellenistic), the better the chances of being democratic.

Based on the polis data (see reference at bottom) it seems there is a direct relationship between the likelihood of a city being a democracy and it's Hellenicity. -- Except for one city of mixed Hellenicity and other cultures and a city of uncertain influence, all known democratic cities were Greek. Though staseisu isn't directly invoked in this hypothesis, further research into the connection between civil war and a city's democracy would be warranted. NOTE: In order to thin a crowded map, only cities with a controlling area of 100 square km or more, are displayed.

City Color Key:

Democracy: Red = 0 (unknown), Blue = 1 (no democracy), Green = 2 (had democracy)

Hellenicity: Red = 0 (unknown), Blue = 1 (most Greek), Green = 2 (mixed), Orange = 3 (most barbarian)
